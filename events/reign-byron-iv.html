reign of king byron iv

type: government
nations: greater sholtercrest
start: harvestgain 23, 125 RR
end: frostfall 16, 173 RR

Byron IV's reign was short for some and long for others. The fact most noted by history is that his death lead with some directness toward the succession struggle now termed the Indigo and Violet War. While Byron himself certainly had nothing to do with those most unfortunate happenings, his lack of clarity about his intended heir contributed.
